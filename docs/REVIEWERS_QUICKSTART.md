# Reviewers quickstart

The system assigns you a random nickname, for example 🫑🐯🔴 (for now we cannot set ones of our choices). If you feel uncomfortable with what the system chosen for you, please let me know.

I, Veelkoov, am not a native English speaker. I am more of an "introvert" person. I often demonstrate "a programmer way of communicating". If anything is unclear, if you want more details - please ask!

I live in central Europe, and work in an office, so I tend to work on getfursu.it on weekends and evenings, including responding to messages.

The system which we will be using is currently **very simple and limited in features**. That's because this is an experiment, and I didn't want to invest much time into something which may not succeed. The software may be improved, possibly often based on your feedback, depending on how the experiment goes.

The documents/information you may find helpful is:
- https://getfursu.it/queue
- [REVIEWS overview and checklist](./REVIEWS.md)

Reviews system is available here after logging in: https://getfursu.it/submissions/1/

In one sentence, as a reviewer, you will be navigating the list of submissions, and for each of them going through the checklist, verifying, commenting, and voting.
